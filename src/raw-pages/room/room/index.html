OUR ROOMS

Our private villa, decorated in a rustic Mexican style, features 5 spacious bedrooms; each bedroom has a king-size bed, private bathroom, air conditioning, ceiling fan, minibar, vanity mirror, hairdryer, towels, and daily housekeeping service.

RATES
Indivial room rate is based on single or double occupancy.
Full villa rate is for 10 people occupancy.
